FAQ

What?

Confinder is a client-side app that lists various pony conventions and finds the ones closest to you.

Why?

I wanted to learn Angular.js and this was the least worst idea I could come up with. ¯\_(ツ)_/¯

How?

Either click the big green Geolocate button, or enter a place and hit submit. You can sort with the sort buttons, and search with the search field. Nifty, huh?

Who?

This thing was made by James Liu, some nerdy guy on the internet.

Where? When?

I really don't know how these could possibly be questions you'd ask about this little thing, but I wanted to round out the set.
